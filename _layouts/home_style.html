Welcome to M.I.S. MODEL BALIKA INTER COLLEGE

The M.I.S. MODEL BALIKA INTER COLLEGE is a girls' secondary school located at Mohalla Kot, Khurja, Dist. Bulandshahr. It is affiliated with Uttar Pradesh (UP) State Board under school code 1154 and UDISE code 09112600113. It is well-known for its consistent excellent academic performance. Students of the M.I.S College have consistently given top-notch performances by securing good grades at the U.P. board high school and intermediate examinations.

Founded in 1993, the school believes in encouraging intellectual curiosity and a lifelong journey of learning. It offers excellent education to approximately 650 students who come from all around the city.
