Smt. Naseem

Principal

Passionate about the education of girls, Mrs Naseem is an accomplished educator whose career has spanned more than 15 years. She holds Master of Arts (M.A.) in History, and Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.). Throughout her career, Mrs Naseem has advocated strongly for the education and equality of girls, and believes firmly in the importance of leadership, training and 'student voice' for girls..


Principal's Message: Welcome to the MIS School, a place of learning and enquiry, where your daughter will be nurtured, challenged and provided with every opportunity to access her full potential. Academic rigour is valued at MIS, but our curriculum is broad and encourages all areas of creativity and innovation. From our earliest learners to our senior girls, we want our students to be the best that they can be.
